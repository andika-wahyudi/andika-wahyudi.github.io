    MELT 2025

    Term 3

    As a member of the Language Development Centre, I contributed to organizing the Matsanewa English Language Tournament (MELT) 2025, a national English competition for elementary school students across Indonesia. I worked with fellow committee members to coordinate event preparations, support participants, and ensure the smooth execution of competition activities, including the English Olympiad and Storytelling Contest. Despite balancing academic responsibilities and organizational commitments, we collaborated effectively to deliver a successful event that promoted English language learning and provided a valuable platform for young learners to showcase their talents.

    Safari Ramadan

    Term 3

    As the Head of Public Relations of the Student Councillor, I led the publicity and communication efforts for the Safari Ramadan programme at Masjid Al Firdaus, Wagir. I coordinated with committee members, promoted the event to participants, and ensured smooth communication between the school, community members, and stakeholders. Despite balancing academic responsibilities and leadership commitments, I worked closely with the organizing team to support activities such as religious talks and the distribution of iftar meals. Through this experience, I strengthened my leadership and teamwork skills while helping to foster connections between our school and the local community.

    Boarder's Outbound Activity

    Term 3

    As the Secretary of the Boarder Council, I helped organize the Boarder’s Outbound Activity at a reservoir in Kepanjen, Malang. I was responsible for coordinating administrative matters, preparing schedules, recording meeting discussions, and ensuring that all necessary information was communicated effectively to participants and committee members. Despite managing academic responsibilities alongside organizational duties, I worked closely with the team to ensure the activity ran smoothly. Through various team-building and outdoor challenges, the programme strengthened the bonds among boarders and created a memorable experience for everyone involved.
