    My Leadership Vision

    My vision as a student leader is to help build a positive, inclusive and supportive school community where every student feels valued and confident to express themselves. I believe that leadership is not about power or status, but about serving others, setting a good example, and working together to create meaningful change. A good leader should be responsible, approachable and willing to listen to the voices of others.

    As a Student Councillor, I hope to act as a bridge between students and the school by listening to my peers' concerns, understanding their perspectives and communicating their ideas respectfully. At the same time, I want to encourage students to take initiative and contribute actively to the school community instead of remaining passive. When students feel that their opinions matter, they are more likely to participate and take pride in their school.

    I also hope to promote values such as teamwork, empathy and resilience among my peers. Through collaboration with fellow councillors and teachers, I aim to organize activities and initiatives that strengthen school spirit and foster stronger relationships within the school community. Ultimately, I hope to inspire others to step forward, support one another and grow together, creating a school environment where everyone feels motivated to learn, lead and succeed.
